MvLink

MvLink is a personal health and activity monitoring application that helps users stay on track with wellness goals through automated SMS notifications.

SMS Notification Consent

By opting in to MvLink notifications, you consent to receive automated SMS messages including:

  • Health and activity reminders
  • Sleep and wake time alerts
  • Daily wellness check-ins

Message frequency: Up to 100 messages per month.

Message and data rates may apply.

Opt-Out

Reply STOP to any message to unsubscribe at any time. You will receive a confirmation message and no further messages will be sent.

Help

Reply HELP to any message for assistance, or contact us at the email below.

Privacy

We do not share your phone number or personal data with third parties. Your information is used solely for delivering notifications you have opted in to receive.
